\bfr{Your Brain}
\bi
\li
90\% of the information transmitted to the brain is visual

\li
The human brain can process an image in just 13 milliseconds

\li
50\% of the brain is active in visual processing

\li
Human brains process visuals 60,000 times faster than they do text

\li
93\% of communication is nonverbal

\li
We are exposed to 5x more information today than we were in 1986
\ei

\vfill\tiny Sources:
\url{https://blog.csgsolutions.com/15-statistics-prove-power-data-visualization}
\end{frame}

\bfr{Communication}
\bi
\li
High quality infographics are 30x more likely to be read than plain text

\li
People who follow directions with illustrations do 323\% better than those who follow text-only directions

\li
If a scientific claim is presented in pure words or numbers, 68\% of people will believe that the information is accurate and truthful. 
But if you put a simple graph with the claim, the number rises to 97\% \

\li
The Wharton School of Business found that while only half of an audience was convinced by a purely verbal presentation, that number jumped to over two thirds when visuals were added

\ei

\vfill\tiny Sources:
\url{https://blog.csgsolutions.com/15-statistics-prove-power-data-visualization}
\end{frame}
